\chapter{Conclusion}

\begin{itemize}
    \item We explored the topic of Salient Object Detection, specifically in the context of Social Media Images.
    \item We reviewed existing methods and models for Salient Object Detection.
    \item We identified the unique challenges posed by Social Media Images, such as diverse content and formats.
